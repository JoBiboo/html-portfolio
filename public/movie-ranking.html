The Best Movies According to Saram

My top 3 movies of all time.

Stardust

This is my FAVORITE movie! It's so much fun to watch. The character development of the main character shows him going from nerdy turd to nerdy and suave.

Beauty and the Beast

Belle is my favorite princess. She was the first one to not fall in love with a handsome man.

The Fellowship of the Ring

A love of Hobbits, Elves, adventure, and magic. What more could you ask for in a movie.
